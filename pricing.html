Pricing FAQs

What counts as a published job post?

A published job post is one that has been displayed on your company job board. Your subscription is automatically updated each time you publish a new job post or archive an active one. That way, you only pay for what you actually need.

How many users can I have?

We don't charge per user and there is no limit to the number of users you can invite to your account. With Polymer, it's only the number of published job posts that affects your monthly subscription.

What happens to archived jobs?

When a job post is archived, any time remaining on its subscription is credited back to your account. Although you only pay for published job posts, you can still browse and view all the candidates and documents of any closed job.

What if I have no published jobs?

You always have access to your past job posts, candidates, and documents. Additionally, you can manage your team, job board, and any future job posts.
